Eric McDowell

Eric McDowell grew up on a small farm just outside of New York City. When he was growing up, he always wanted a way to connect with his friends. Throughout high school Eric thrived all around, but took to the digital arts. He trained hard at Caltech to eventually become one of Microsoft's forefront web designers. While working at Microsoft he helped the "connections" division, always allowing Microsoft to connect to their users. Now he resides in Toronto, Canada, where he designed the InstantGramme website.
